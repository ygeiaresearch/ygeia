What is Ygeia Research?

Ygeia is a nonprofit of chemists, chemical engineers, and software engineers, working to solve the water crisis.

  Open Source
All code for projects is open source at GitHub under the MIT License, and all of our designs are under the Creative Commons License.
  Research
We aim to be a hub for water purification research. We aim to be collaborative and communicative, to make things easier for people to solve water crises in their own communities.
  Affordable or Free Products
Based on our donations, we aim to be a nonprofit that can provide clean water products to those in need for free or for less than $1.

Ygeia @ Github

Ygeia is Free and Open Source Software and Technology for Water Purification

Everything produced by Ygeia and its members and contributors is open source. We are combating the lack of access to information and lack of money constraints in the water crisis.
