Project 1: PotPerm Calculator

This is the Potassium Permanganate calculator, the first project of Ygeia Research. It takes 2 inputs, Fe amount and Mn amount, and outputs a PotPerm amount, using the equation [Mn+2] * 1.92 + [Fe+2] * 0.57 (all concentrations in mg/L)
